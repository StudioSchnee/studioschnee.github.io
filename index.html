WELCOME TO シュニー・スタジオ ｜「SCHNEE・STUDIO」

(It's pronounced "Schnee Dot Studio")

We are an independent development studio specializing in retro console development and reverse engineering. Our goal is to bring relevance to our favorite systems of generations past and to reintroduce current players to games on a widely accessible platform: PC.

To this end, our focus is currently on reverse engineering game binaries on systems such as the Sony PlayStation and Nintendo GameCube. In doing so, we can provide players with the means to play classics never-before-seen on PC (granted that you have the prerequisite data files from your own copy of said game...). We do not provide ANY copyrighted content in our reverse-engineered releases.

To stay in the loop, be sure to check out the news blog, and follow our work on Twitter and GitHub.

Thanks for checking us out!
