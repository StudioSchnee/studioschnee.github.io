About Us

Our Mission

Rediscovering the past, looking to the future, for all to see.

SCHNEE・STUDIO was founded in early 2021 by Michelle and Samantha Schnee with the goal of designing fun and memorable games by analyzing classic titles of generations past with a critical eye and expanding on their ideas while aiming for greater inclusivity and diversity.

Our Philosophy

Each generation is a layer of foundation upon which future games are built. We must understand the foundation laid before us so that the medium can grow. To this end, we seek to create games that build upon the design philosophies of developers who came before us. From story, to art, to music and sound, we aim to provide innovative games targeted at those oft neglected and forgotten by the industry.
