WELCOME TO シュニー・スタジオ ｜「SCHNEE・STUDIO」

(It's pronounced "Schnee Dot Studio")

We are an independent studio specializing in game development on retro systems. Our goal is to pay homage to the consoles of years past by approaching their unique limitations and features with a modern eye and iterating on the ideas of developers at the time. We hope that players across generations will enjoy our work and acquaint/reacquaint themselves with gaming's formative years.

To this end, our current focus is on researching and building upon the shoulders of the giants that came before us, how they bent the rules and skirted around hardware limitations to create some of gaming's greatest hits. We seek to marry their knowledge of their platforms with current design philosophies and understanding to recapture the magical spark that these old beasts had.

To stay up to date, be sure to check out the news blog, and follow our work on Twitter and GitHub.

Thanks for checking us out!
