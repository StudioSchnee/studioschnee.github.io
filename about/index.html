ABOUT シュニー・スタジオ ｜「SCHNEE・STUDIO」

THE TEAM

シュニー・スタジオ ｜「SCHNEE・STUDIO」 is a duo consisting of wives Michelle and Samantha Schnee (Misha and Sammy). We met in 2019 and developed a close bond due to our mutual love for tech and video games. We both share a fiery passion for our work, and it is that passion that drives us to put everything we've got into each project we undertake.

シュニー・スタジオ ｜「SCHNEE・STUDIO」 was founded in 2021 due to our desire to spend our time during the COVID-19 quarantine more productively. Our love for retro media and tech inspired us to pursue game development and design in the pursuit of paying homage to some of our favorites from childhood (Final Fantasy, Metal Gear, Silent Hill, etc.).

We hope you enjoy our work and support our current and future endeavors. Thank you!
